Given X~f(x), ```f(x)``` is the density function of X, e.g. N(0,1).
Sampling X ```N``` times, resulting in x1,x2...xN.
Given ```W```, the minimal width of each bar,
	  we produce a histrogram ```H(x)```.
Then, there's a conclusion: ```H(x)~=f(x)*W*N```.
